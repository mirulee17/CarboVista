What Does CarboVista Do?

CarboVista integrates satellite imagery and machine learning to estimate, analyse, and visualise urban tree carbon stocks at city scale.

Satellite Imagery

Sentinel-2 multispectral imagery captures vegetation reflectance and canopy structure across urban areas.

Machine Learning

An ensemble learning model predicts pixel-level aboveground carbon density from spectral features.

Spatial Visualisation

Carbon estimates are mapped spatially to reveal patterns, hotspots, and urban variability.

Change Detection

Compare urban tree carbon stocks across different time periods to identify gains, losses, and long-term vegetation trends.
